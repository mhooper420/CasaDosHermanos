N3Head-end — the tech closet

Wall board in the Bed-2 walk-in closet: top-down, ONT → UCG-Fiber → USW-Pro-Max-16-PoE → 24-port patch panel → Home Assistant host, UPS on the floor. Conditioned space retires the old garage thermal problem entirely; the switch is fanless and the stack is effectively silent, so it can share a wall with a desk. Feed everything from the new 20 A dedicated circuit through the UPS. Post-close (Layer B): swap the closet door for a louvred one or add a passive vent grille — ~60 W of gear in a sealed cupboard wants an air path. The attic conduit stub lands here, which makes every attic-reachable point in the house permanently upgradeable from the head-end.
